In paree

In paree, paree,
what the lovers do see, in paree.
the moonlight shines through their eyes, in paree.
In paree, paree.

In paree, paree,
the lovers hearts dance in paree.
Moments last an eternity in paree,
In paree, paree.

In paree, paree
the lovers experience in paree.
the candles of love flicker in paree.
In paree, paree.

In paree, paree,
what the lovers do see, in paree.
the moonlight shines through their eyes, in paree.
In paree, paree.

In paree, paree,
the lovers hearts dance in paree.
Moments last an eternity in paree,
In paree, paree.

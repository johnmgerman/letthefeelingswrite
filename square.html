You can be the squarest of squares
and you shouldn't carest of cares.
Yourself is beautiful
with who you are.
Feeling the feels
with no hint of an emotional scar.
Day upon day
with wonder in your soul.
The squarest can be the carest,
through a wondrous life's stroll.
Day after day
night after night.
You no longer know
like you are some fright.
Dare to be
what you know you see;
and proudly be a square
with yourself no longer to unbare.

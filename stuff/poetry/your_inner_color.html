When life seems to cast you out,
and you don't fit in.
When the world seems colorless,
even when the rainbow comes out.
When the wind blows the trash,
leaving the world a mess.
When the leaves start to wither,
like the thoughts dreaming of doubt.

None of that matters,
none of that changes
all of you,
that you have,
inside ...

Always remember,
the shades of life,
never dull
your inner color.

An image of pure joy,
appears every day.
Soulfully lost in the,
purest of forms.
Hate, bitterness, shame,
all nowhere to be found.
Headphones on, happiness on,
dancing within gratefulness.
Others, zombe-like,
emotionless.
Like starlight in the darkness,
even in day time.
Music from the soul shines out,
as music from without shines in.
a oneness,
a togetherness,
within themselves,
and within life.
Embracing joy,
emitting joy ...
being joy.

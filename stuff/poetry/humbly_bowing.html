Sometimes I look through the eyes of the darkness,
rushing through the ocean of rippling stars.

Eyes of the universe blinking with the hint of day,
my mind blistfully unaware of the vastness that I see.

Riding the tracks of the path of this planet,
I gaze out of the greatest looking glass called earth.

You barely see anything, but you see all of everything,
a spec of life in the body of the universe.

Humbly bowing to the sights up above,
gloriously feeling the humility of the views of the eyes
from within each other.

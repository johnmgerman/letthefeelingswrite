A man's hand extended, nonexistent to those who walk by.
Eyes shattered of hope.
The thoughts of tomorrow become a mere distant dream of his current reality.
A life barely known to be alive.
Until one-day passer byers abruptly halt their strides. Their eyes gaze into themselves.
A once unnoticed hand becomes their own.
Everything that was seems different.
Though everything that is is all the same; except for the homeless man's face that is now hidden behind a mirror.

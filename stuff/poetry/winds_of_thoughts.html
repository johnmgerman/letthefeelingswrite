The waves of the mind come crashing,
as the thoughts of you come flooding through.
Happily restless,
Mindfully blistfull,
How enchanting the world is with those thoughts rippling around.

Never dissipating, unwavering like time crossing the ocean.
The winds of thoughts sail with you.
Times, days, seasons circulating around each other.
Mind, spirit, soul floating through the winds of thoughts.

Exquisitely deserted,
replenished through the oasis of your soul.
Grand ocean of memories,
reaching further then any depth.

Beautifully serene,
swimming through tranquility.

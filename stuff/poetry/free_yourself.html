A child-like imagination
grows from within.
Knowledge blossoms
with excitement.
Wondrous what if's
erupt.

Doubtful uncertainty begins
to creep through.
Wondrous what if's morph
to negative what if's.
Ignorance's fears enhance
the negativity,
wanting to weigh you down.
Wanting to trap you,
while knowledge wants to
free you.

Never surrender your
thoughts to the limits ignorance
wants you to think you have.
Free yourself.
Free your imagination.
Free to be limitless.

Desperate eyes fixate on anything.
Eyes as lonely as she feels.
Loneliness at a table
yet surrounded by life.
One chair,
One her,
emptiness engulfs from within.
Face of a life unnoticed.
Seemingly so black and white
yet so colorfully disorganized.
Who will fill her cup of life?
Who will be her hero
and wear the cape?
To fill a life unnoticed.
To colorfully change her mood.
Be the hero,
wear the cape,
by acknowledging
a life unnoticed
with a simple ...
hello.

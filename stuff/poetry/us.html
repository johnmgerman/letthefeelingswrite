Rain drops glisten,
natures listen.
hearts beating,
loves first meeting.

Souls combine,
on eternities line.

Forever flame,
together, never the same.

Quest for loves clock strikes rescind,
unconditional love now pinned.

2 to 1,
glow of warmth like the sun.

1 soul, 1 being,
soulful hearts together seeing.

To never extinguiish, to never fade,
from the beginning, for each other,
we were made.

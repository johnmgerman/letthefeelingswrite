Nothing else on this earthly realm,
compares to the feelings of your soulful touch.
The winds of eternity,
dance and swirl around each other.
Embracing the elements of life,
embracing the elements of our souls.
The hands of the hearts,
rhythmically intertwined.
Glorious sunshine bursts through and out,
through the inner self.
Never wanting to let go,
never wanting to be unchained.
An artistic masterpiece,
of two.
The hands of eternity,
touching always,
never unchained,
embracing the artistic masterpiece,
of two.

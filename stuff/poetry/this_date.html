As time passes,
our lives intertwine with our souls.
You, I, eternity.
Everything that was, is,
and everything that was never, will be.
Everything that did, will.
everything that didn't, shall.
Waltzing through life,
spiritually embracing.
A date will be,
a date shall come,
a date we've had,
a date that shall embrace our lives.
A date of foreverness,
a date of togetherness.
A date,
one date,
a date ...
We've had this date with each other
from the beginning.

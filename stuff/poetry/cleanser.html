Cleaning upon cleaning,
scrub a dub dub,
everything gets clean,
but remains dirty nonetheless.

New sprays, different sprays,
new scrubs, different scrubs.
Everything upon everything,
cleanly dirty.

No matter how much you try
to clean your sins,
the house of your soul
remains a mess.

The dirt, the mess,
the sin ...
There is only one way to cleanse
the house of your soul.
Soulfully and spiritually,
through the greatest cleanser of all.

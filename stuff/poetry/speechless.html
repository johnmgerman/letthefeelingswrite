Visions of her dance and
sing within my mind.
Like the statue of David,
so is she to a woman.
Her eyes captivate with one look.
All the essence of life is
encapsulated in her beauty.
One look,
One glance,
everything and all ... speechless.
Heart feeling the words,
Mind singing the thoughts.
Lips ... speechless.
One thought of her,
one look of her ...
everything and all ... speechless.
May hearts do the talking,
May lips remain.
May the world become as speechless
as the visions of her to me.

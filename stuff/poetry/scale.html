When you come to me, truth is what I always hope to give you,
never a lie, as that is always something I hope not to.
I am never here to embarrass you with whatever I tell,
always respectful of you and that will always ring like a bell.
I am one who will never, ever judge,
when we are together, and you smile, it gives my heart a loving nudge.
Any time you are welcome around anywhere,
To weigh our thoughts, only between us we will share.
For you, I will do whatever I can not to fail,
wouldn't it be great if everyone behaved like a scale?

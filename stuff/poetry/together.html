A competition does not need to be.
A fact, at times, that humans do not see.
Inside themselves, they might think what they know is more true,
but what they find within each other, they might find more of what they think too.
When we allow ourselves to think only of what we know,
our own stubborness becomes our own intellects foe.
When we allow ourselves to intertwine and all we know become one,
the warmth we can give out comes from our own inner sun.
Let go of what you think and become what we can find,
as we all, together, illuminate the future of mankind.

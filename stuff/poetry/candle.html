A simpler, more peaceful time I bring to you,
the feelings of rest I hope to glow through.
I'm as common as most you can find,
but my warmth I hope to leave behind.
Even though my help may seem to diminish more and more,
I'll continue like it was all from before.
Waht a lovely scent we can bring to each other's lives,
like the intoxicating perfumed thoughts of all loving husband's wives.
Let the flickering of kinsmanship never be something we allow to mishandle,
wouldn't it be great if everyone behaved like a candle?

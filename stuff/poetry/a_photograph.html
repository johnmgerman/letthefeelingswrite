Beauty in your eyes.
softness of your soul.
Timelessness of you.
Clock stops, forever starts.
Nowhere a blemish.
Natural exquisitemness
captured in one.
Beauty of sunset
captured in your smile.
Every blink snaps a picture
of the beauty of you.
Lens of the eyes
always focused.
Beauty of you
never ending.
Search no more
perfect shot is you.
Wherever you are
the perfect shot
includes you.
Universal love
and beauty
encapsulated
within and without
all of you.
A photograph of you,
a photograph of pure beauty.

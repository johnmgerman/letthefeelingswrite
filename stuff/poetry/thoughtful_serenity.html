Our thoughts can feel like they were love.

A waterfall of heartfulness can flow down into our souls.

Rippling time hears the words of our eternity.

Wondrously enchanted, our emotions conduct with a melodic purity.

Mindfulness appears peacefully, flickering through the candlelight of our minds.

Calmly resting, tucked within the serenity of the comforter of conscieceness.

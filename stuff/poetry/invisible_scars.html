A beating here, a beating there,
scars, scars - everywhere.

There lasting strikes seem to never fade,
a beaten soul, forever made?

Our minds, our bodies, are in tune,
an understanding never comes too soon.

When we give in to our hateful side,
silent tears can never be heard to have cried.

Mental scars may not be shown,
from within, they can be forever known.

Just because the scars can't be seen,
doesnn't mean they can't be felt just as mean.

Time though does give us to start anew,
to brighten the days that were once blue.

To change yourself, and change a letter,
to help make the world all that much better.

Twinkle, twinkle the invisible scars,
what a wonder, filling our hearts with stars.

When we know, what we can see,
a different world, from within and without, will truly be set free.

Within the darkness we can become our own bright, shiny star,
instead of remaining our own life's scar.
The truth to a better world lies within one another,
to see each, as if, we were each others brother.
Loving each other enough to show eacha other what to do,
to bring up, so not a crowd could give a single boo.
All throughout life we would help, as if we were the same,
raining down to darken the darkened flame.
With a heart that sees with the vision of all,
so the truth within one another can help us overcome any wall.

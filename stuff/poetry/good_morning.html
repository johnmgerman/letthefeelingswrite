Ocean-like colors coming from above,
spreading across the morning sky.
Washing away the twinkling of the stars,
washing ashore the sun-like waves.
Caressing warmth from the suns
extended hands.
Cheerfully greeting the rejuvenation
of nature's life.
Happiness from above,
smiling down with its illuminating love.
Awakening with a glowful light,
nature's alarm clock warmly
says Good Morning.

As the wars rage on,
and the storms surround my life,
if that emant you were safe,
and you are sound,
I wouldn't have changed that
for anything, nowing you have
a smile on your face and in your
soul.

Thank God you weren't around.

While life continues to try and rip apart
everything I say and do, knowing
that eternal peace surrounds
you and within, brings me more earthly
joy and happiness within my soul

Thank God you weren't around.

I know that sounds odd and
most probably won't understand,
but your happiness to me means
everything even if that means
I can't have much of anything.

Unconditional love isn't about
physical closeness, that's why
I can say, even though strange, ...

Thank God you weren't around.

The wars raged on, the storms
thundered, but knowing you were
safe and sound, peace
all around ...

Thank God you weren't around.

I'll give you a place where you can lie down,
hopefully there is never a moment where your heart does frown.
Rest and relaxation is what I will provide,
hopefully you have enough room to stretch out wide.
Every moment I want you to feel the coziness of a princess bride,
so not a thought ever comes like someone is not on your side.
Plush emotions, may they come forevermore,
may my presence fill you with those senses galore.
I hope my comfort becomes like being eternally wed,
wouldn't it be great if everyone behaved like a bed?

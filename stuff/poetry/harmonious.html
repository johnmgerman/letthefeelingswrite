A rainbow of colors,
not a one looked at differently.
Beautifully intertwined,
harmoniously as one.

Appearance illuminates the spirit,
any color diminishes nothing.
Negativity disappears,
positivity surrounds.

A spectrum of colors,
teaching humanity how to be.
Nature's vision,
sees all as meaningful.

If only humans could see
through nature's vision.
Each color beautifully chosen,
each human beautifully and
colorfully meaningful.

To become the change,
and help others become
the change,
you can find change
within the word itself.

C - Correction:  
Find the correction within yourself that you want to see in the change you want.
H - Help:          
Help others, help you, help others find the change within themselves.
A - Always:      
Never give up. Always be the change you want.
N - Novice:       
Remain thinking like a novice; Continue the curiosity that helped you want to become, be and see the change.
G - Giver:         
Be willing to give to the change, not just be the receiver of the change.
E - Excitement:
Remember why you began. Keep the excitement from when you first felt you wanted the change.

Everyone can be the change,
just need to remain
the change.

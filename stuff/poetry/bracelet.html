Bracelet of life,
bracelet of love.
wrapped around
our souls.
emotions to display,
emotions to remember.
eternally conscience,
heartfully awakened.

A constant feeling,
unconditionally heightened.
A sense of closeness,
from near or far.
Band of togetherness,
bound from within.

Let the unconditionalness
be our support
and become the
brace of our lives.

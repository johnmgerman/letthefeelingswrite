Violets sing, greens bloom.
yellows shine, blues calm.
reds love, browns natural.
white cleanliness, black elegance.
Colors of our existence
of what we could be.
The love of the song of our hearts.
The shine of our elegance.
The natural calm of our thoughts.
The cleanliness of our souls as we bloom into the newness of forever.
May we green with violets.
May we yellow with blues.
May we find brown reds.
And may the white of our eternity always be black.
May the trueness of ourselves
colorfully find its way out.

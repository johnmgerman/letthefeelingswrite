Natures Hope

Silently picturesque of the evenings sunset,
a calming stillness erupts around all of it.
Like a setting where two people could have first met,
two empty chairs left for two people who just fit.


Vibrant views of a lake made for just two people,
natures hope for them is to end near a steeple.
What a way for nature to help someone to thrive,
two empty chairs, sitting, yet soulfully alive.
